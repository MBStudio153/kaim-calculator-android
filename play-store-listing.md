## Full Description (4000 char max)

KAIM Calculator brings the Knowledge Asset Impairment Model to your pocket.

KAIM treats a company's R&D department as its factory floor — output measured in the cash returns it generates, not the year the expense hit the income statement. Most companies' R&D investment is expensed immediately under GAAP, which means decades of value-creating work never shows up on the balance sheet. KAIM restates that value and shows you what book value — and price-to-book — look like once it's recognized.

WHAT IT DOES
• Enter any public company's GAAP shareholders' equity, market cap, and R&D spend
• Choose Simplified mode for a quick estimate, or Full Model mode to build a year-by-year R&D ledger
• Apply the model's three impairment triggers (innovation stagnation, platform abandonment, competitive displacement) to test whether a company's knowledge base still holds its stated value
• Layer in forward contract assets — hardware/chip purchase commitments and cloud service agreements — the same way the model treats them as productive capital
• See your company's restated KAIM Book Value and KAIM Price-to-Book side-by-side with the standard GAAP figures
• Works for annual or quarterly reporting periods

WHO IT'S FOR
Investors, analysts, students, and anyone who wants a second lens on how the market is pricing a tech or R&D-heavy company beyond what GAAP book value shows.

PRIVACY
KAIM Calculator runs entirely on your device. No data is collected, transmitted, or stored on any server. Nothing you enter ever leaves your phone.

DISCLAIMER
KAIM Calculator is an independent, unofficial implementation of the KAIM methodology described in "Evaluating Tech Stocks Using the Knowledge Asset Impairment Model" by Matt Bernau. It is provided for educational and illustrative purposes only and does not constitute financial, investment, legal, or accounting advice. KAIM is a research lens, not an audited accounting standard. Consult a licensed financial advisor and audited financial statements before making any investment decision.
